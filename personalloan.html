Personal Loans

Complete guide to Personal Loans in India

What is a Personal Loan?

A personal loan is an unsecured loan offered by banks and NBFCs for personal needs such as medical emergencies, travel, education, weddings, home renovation, or debt consolidation. No collateral is required to avail a personal loan.

Common Uses of Personal Loan

Medical emergencies
Weddings & events
Travel & vacation
Education expenses
Debt consolidation
Home renovation

Loan Amount & Interest

  • Loan Amount: ₹25,000 up to ₹40 Lakhs (varies by lender)
  • Interest Rate: Typically 9% – 25% per annum
  • Loan tenure depends on lender & applicant profile

Personal Loan Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 21 – 60 years
  • Employment: Salaried or Self-employed
  • Minimum monthly income: ₹15,000+
  • Credit score: 700+ recommended
  • EMI burden should be below 40% of income

Documents Required

  • PAN Card & Aadhaar Card
  • Address proof
  • Salary slips (last 3–6 months)
  • Bank statements (last 6 months)
  • Employment or business proof
