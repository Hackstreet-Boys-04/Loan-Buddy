What is a Business Loan?

A business loan is a financial product offered by banks and financial institutions to support business-related needs such as expansion, working capital, equipment purchase, inventory management, or starting a new business. Business loans may be secured or unsecured depending on the lender and loan amount.

Types of Business Loans

Term Loan – Long-term expansion or asset purchase
Working Capital Loan – Daily business operations
MSME / SME Loan – For small & medium enterprises
Equipment / Machinery Loan – Purchase of machinery
Business Line of Credit – Flexible borrowing
Startup Loan – For new businesses

Documents Required

  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Voter ID)
  • Address Proof (Utility bills, Rental agreement)
  • Business Proof (GST, Shop Act, Partnership Deed)
  • Income Proof (IT Returns – last 2–3 years)
  • Bank Statements (Last 6–12 months)
  • Financial Statements (P&L, Balance Sheet)
  • Passport-size photographs
