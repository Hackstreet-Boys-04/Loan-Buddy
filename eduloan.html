About Educational Loan

Educational loans help students pursue higher studies in India or abroad. These loans cover tuition fees, hostel charges, books, travel expenses, and other education-related costs.

Eligible Courses

  • Graduate & Postgraduate Degrees
  • Professional Courses (Engineering, Medical, MBA, MCA)
  • Chartered Accountancy (CA), ICWA, CFA
  • Studies Abroad (MS, MBA, PhD, etc.)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicant must be an Indian citizen
  • Admission secured in a recognized institution
  • Age: No strict age limit
  • Co-applicant required (Parent / Guardian)
  • Good academic record preferred

Expenses Covered

  • Tuition & hostel fees
  • Books, equipment, laptop
  • Exam & laboratory fees
  • Travel expenses for abroad studies

Documents Required

  • Admission letter from institution
  • Academic certificates
  • Aadhaar & PAN card
  • Income proof of co-applicant
  • Bank statements
